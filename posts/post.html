We traversed a trackless waste of moss which, bending to the pressure of broad tire or padded foot, rose up again behind us, leaving no sign that we had passed. We might indeed have been the wraiths of the departed dead upon the dead sea of that dying planet for all the sound or sign we made in passing. It was the firstmarch of a large body of men and animals I had ever witnessed which raised no dust and left no words of spoor; for there is no dust upon Mars except in the cultivated districts during the winter months, and even then the absence of high winds.
